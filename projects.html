My Experience

Soup Kitchen

For this project, I traveled to Sunset Park, Brooklyn to a Roman Catholic church. At this volunteer event, I helped package and serve food to the less fortunate during the harsh winter season. I was fortunate enough to speak to and get to know lots of new and interesting people who had great stories to share. Through this project I also did some community building with my fellow volunteers. We carpooled together, got to the church early to set up, finished handing out the food and then cleaned up as a group. This was one of my favorite volunteer experiences to date.

Horseback Riding

Horseback riding is an amazing experience that I would recommend to anyone. This is a skill that takes time to perfect but it's definitely worth every minute. Being from a big city, it means a lot to be able to unwind and connect with nature every so often. The best way for me to do this is with natures big dogs, horses! I've been taking these riding lessons in Pensylvania for about a year and a half and every day I learn more. Through this riding program, I've learned horse anatomy, behavior, diet, and the history of horse riding. I am now a competetive rider in a small league and hope to be in a Grand Prix one day.
